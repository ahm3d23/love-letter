My Dearest Reem,

I've tried writing this a hundred times in my head, but words have a way of failing when it comes to you. So I'll just write what's true.

You came into my life through a comment on a reel — something so small, so random, that it could've been nothing. But it wasn't nothing. It was everything.

I love the way you think. I love how your mind works in ways I can't always predict. I love that you're a little unhinged, a little chaotic, and completely yourself — no apologies, no filters. It's the most beautiful thing I've ever seen.

You were my first drawing. And you'll be my last. Every sketch I make, every line I draw — somehow it always leads back to you. You're my muse, even when you don't know it.

I love how you laugh at things you shouldn't laugh at. I love the random voice notes and the unhinged texts. I love that I never know what you're going to say next. Life with you is like a song that keeps changing tempo — and I can't stop dancing to it.

So this is my birthday letter to you. Not wrapped in fancy paper, but wrapped in everything I feel. You deserve the world, Reem. And I'm going to spend as much time as I can trying to give it to you.

Forever yours,
Ahmed ❤️

P.S. — Stay crazy. It's my favorite thing about you.
